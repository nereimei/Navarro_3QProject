Who and What We Are

This is Environment in Detriment, at Risk, or ENDER for short. ENDER is a non profit orgainization with the goal of promoting awareness and action towards the effects of climate change on the concerning health of the human population.

Why Should You Join

If you can read this, then you are human. Matters concerning physical health also concern you. Climate change leaves lasting consequences on the geographical makeup of the world, yet health is commonly overlooked. Be part of the action to save more people and help heal our planet.
